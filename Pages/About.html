This is Our About Page

Capacity Bay, Inc. is an innovative eLearning platform and organization with a mission to revolutionize learning and workforce development. Founded by Obi Nwachukwu, Capacity Bay aims to empower individuals and communities, including U.S. Veterans, transitioning service members, New Americans, immigrants, and refugees. Their practical, hands-on approach simplifies online learning, making it accessible from the comfort of home or office. Notably, they extend their impact to technology-deprived communities, such as sub-Saharan Africa. In addition to training, Capacity Bay provides integrated IT consulting services for individuals, businesses, and government agencies. Since its inception in 2014, Capacity Bay has been a trailblazer in information technology training.Since its formation, Capacity Bay, Inc. has made meaningful strides as an innovative leader in the areas of information technology training.
